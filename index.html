Equivalence of diploma in Cameroon :

The equivalence of the diploma in Cameroun is exclusively reserved to the National Commission for the Evaluation of Training Offered Abroad (CNE) which, after having received a file of a candidate through the SUCEA ( Sub Department of comparative university systems and Academiques equivalences), meets in ordinary session each three (3) month (quarter) or as a need, in extraordinary session under the coordination of the Minister for the higher education. It is this commission (CNE) which submits a project of decree of equivalence to the Minister for the higher education...

Equivalence on the platform :

The equivalence of the diploma on this platform is based on all the result that the National Commission for the Evaluation of Training Offered Abroad (CNE) have published since the date of his creation. In other hand we give you the possibility to have an approximate equivalence based on some calculations and probabilities in the case which your diploma is not yet evaluated by the CNE.
After each three (3) month (quarter) new equivalences are available depending on the last result of the CNE that the Minister for the higher education have published.
